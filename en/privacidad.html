Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit certain web pages. They allow, among other things, information about browsing habits or the device itself to be stored and retrieved, and may be used to recognise the user.

9.2. Types of cookies used on this Website

The Website may use the following categories of cookies:

  • Technical or necessary cookies: essential for correct Website operation and do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies: allow measurement and statistical analysis of Website usage.
  • Functional cookies: remember optional preferences to provide a more personalised experience.
  • Marketing cookies: allow content or communications to be adapted to your interests.

Analytics, functional and marketing cookies will only be activated if you give consent through the initial settings screen or any cookie management tools made available on the Website.

You can configure your cookie preferences from the settings interface available on the Website and may modify or withdraw your consent at any time.
